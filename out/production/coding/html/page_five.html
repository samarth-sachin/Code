Bluetooth Tracing

The Bluetooth Tracing feature allows you to detect nearby Bluetooth-enabled devices. This can be helpful for tracking lost devices or ensuring safety by monitoring for unknown devices in your vicinity.

Steps to use Bluetooth Tracing:

  1. Turn on Bluetooth on your device.
  2. Click the "Scan Devices" button below to start scanning.
  3. A list of nearby Bluetooth devices will be displayed.
